### B. Plant Cell Structure
**4. Labeling Functions:** Match the plant cell part to its correct function. **[6 marks]**
   *   **Cell Wall**
   *   **Cell Membrane**
   *   **Cytoplasm**
   *   **Nucleus**
   *   **Chloroplast**
   *   **Sap Vacuole**

   *Functions:*
   *   i. Controls what goes in and out of the cell.
   *   ii. Contains a green pigment called chlorophyll; makes food for the plant.
   *   iii. A large fluid-filled space that helps keep the cell firm.
   *   iv. The "brain" of the cell; controls the cell's activities.
   *   v. A tough, stiff outer layer made of cellulose that holds the plant's shape.
   *   vi. A clear jelly-like substance where chemical reactions happen to keep the cell alive.
