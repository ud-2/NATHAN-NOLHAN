### SECTION 1: Number Operations and Properties (12 Marks)
*This section tests your understanding of integers, roots, fractions, decimals, and divisibility.*

**Question 1: Integers and Roots**

c) Find two consecutive integers that $\sqrt{85}$ lies between. **[1 mark]**

**Question 2: Fractions and Decimals**  
a) Calculate $2\frac{1}{4} + 1\frac{2}{3}$. Give your answer as a mixed number in its simplest form. **[3 marks]**  
b) Calculate $4.5 \times 0.8$ **[2 marks]**

**Question 3: Factors and Multiples**  
a) Find the Highest Common Factor (HCF) of $18$ and $24$. **[1 mark]**

### SECTION 5: Geometry, Measure and Probability (13 Marks)
*This section tests your knowledge of 2D/3D shapes, area, volume, and chance.*

**Question 12: Area and Volume**  
a) A triangle has a base of $14 \text{ cm}$ and a perpendicular height of $8 \text{ cm}$. Calculate the area of the triangle. **[2 marks]**  
b) A solid cuboid has a length of $5 \text{ cm}$, a width of $3 \text{ cm}$, and a height of $10 \text{ cm}$. Calculate the volume of the cuboid. **[2 marks]**

**Question 13: Probability**  
A fair ten-sided spinner is numbered from $1$ to $10$. The spinner is spun once.  
a) Write down the probability that the spinner lands on the number $7$. **[1 mark]**  
b) Write down the probability that the spinner lands on an **even number**. **[1 mark]**  
c) Write down the probability that the spinner lands on a **prime number**. *(Hint: 1 is not a prime number).* **[2 marks]**  
d) Are the events "landing on an even number" and "landing on a multiple of 5" mutually exclusive? Give a reason for your answer. **[2 marks]**
